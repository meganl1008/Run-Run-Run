Boston Marathon Analysis

The Boston Marathon is one of the most popular and competitive races in the world. It is typically held on Patriots' Day and its course runs from Hopkinton in southern Middlesex Country to Copley Square in Boston. The event attracts 500,000 spectators each year, making it New England's most widely viewed sporting event.Â The event now attracts an average of about 30,000 registered participants each year, with over 30,000 individuals entering the race.
